About lojinha! (mini-store)

This mini-store has two purposes:

  • test technologies like Akka 2 and the Play Framework 2
  • sell (for real) things I don't use or want anymore

If you want to know more about the first item, take a look at the source code in my github: github.com/jcranky/lojinha

Otherwise, this is how the bidding works. First, its an informal auction, without pressure for me to sell or you to buy. Make your bid and, if its a fair one, I'll get in touch so that we can discuss the details. Values in Reals please (or contact me directly).

About the delivery, I live in Mogi das Cruzes. That means I can deliver in person around here. I can also deliver in person in SP, since I have to go there from time to time. For other cities I'll mail the items, but the mail fare is on the buyer.

You can also contact me directly. I'm jcranky on twitter and github, and my site is jcranky.com. So it is not hard to find me.

thanks!
